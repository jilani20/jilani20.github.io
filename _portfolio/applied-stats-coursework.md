Foundational statistics and ML coursework, completed as part of a Data Science and Business Analytics program. Each project applied a distinct technique to a real business-style dataset:

- **New Wheels** (SQL) — fetching, filtering, and aggregating sales data; window functions and subqueries to diagnose declining customer retention.
- **Trade&Ahead** (Unsupervised Learning) — K-means and hierarchical clustering to group stocks by financial characteristics, with cluster profiling.
- **EasyVisa** (Ensemble Techniques) — bagging and boosting models with hyperparameter tuning to predict visa approval outcomes.
- **INN Hotels** (Classification) — logistic regression and decision trees (with pruning) to predict booking cancellations.
- **ReCell** (Regression) — linear regression to build a dynamic pricing strategy for used devices.
- **E-news Express** (Business Statistics) — hypothesis testing and A/B testing to evaluate a new landing page's effectiveness.
- **FoodHub** (Python/EDA) — exploratory data analysis on food delivery order data to surface business insights.

**Stack:** Python, MySQL, scikit-learn, pandas, statistical testing
